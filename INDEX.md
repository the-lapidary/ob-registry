## NOTES ON REGISTRY COMPLETENESS

The Lapidary holds nine confirmed fragments. The registry does not
claim this is a complete accounting of existing fragments.

Criteria for confirmation: physical verification by a Lapidary-affiliated
holder, mass and optical measurements consistent with known OB properties,
and at minimum one documented transmission to the registry.

Fragments may exist outside the registry. The Lapidary monitors for
indicators of unregistered fragments. No indicators have been acted on
at the time of this filing.

The registry was not started at the beginning. It was started when
someone decided to start it. What preceded the registry is in the BLACKBOOK.
What the BLACKBOOK does not cover is in the space before the BLACKBOOK.
The Lapidary does not have a document for that space.
