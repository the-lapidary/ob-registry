Each claims the other holds either a different fragment (unregistered, potentially significant) or a replica (which the Lapidary considers unlikely given the mass properties, which cannot be replicated by any known means).

The dispute is open. The Lapidary has filed it as CONTESTED rather than UNKNOWN because at least one of the parties has the fragment, and the fragment is confirmed to exist.
