## CROSS-FRAGMENT OBSERVATIONS

OB-07 produces the strongest and most consistent cross-fragment responses of any registered fragment. During each of OB-07's liquefaction events, the following was observed in other registered fragments:

- OB-01: Temperature drop 1.4°C–2.3°C (increasing with each successive event)
- OB-02: Temperature drop 1.1°C (Event 4 only — no response to Events 1–3)
- OB-03: Temperature drop 0.6°C–0.8°C
- OB-05: Temperature drop 1.9°C–3.7°C (most responsive non-Class I fragment)
- OB-06: No measurable response (consistent across all four events)

The Lapidary notes that OB-06 — the fragment with the highest mass-to-volume ratio — has never responded to another fragment's event. The Lapidary does not have a hypothesis for this.
