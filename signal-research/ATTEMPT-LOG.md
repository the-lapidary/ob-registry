## CROSS-FRAGMENT RESONANCE — MECHANISM UNKNOWN

*Filed as a separate section, not an attempt*

During OB-05's third liquefaction event (spontaneous), the following was observed across the fragment network:

- OB-01 (approximately 4,000km from OB-05): temperature drop 1.4°C
- OB-07 (several thousand km from OB-05): temperature drop 3.7°C
- OB-03 (continental Europe): temperature drop 0.8°C

Duration of each cross-fragment response: matched OB-05 event duration exactly.

The Lapidary notes: spontaneous liquefaction events produce cross-fragment resonance. Induced attempts (Attempts 004–008) have not produced this resonance, even when responses were observed in the inducted fragment.

This suggests the cross-fragment resonance is a property of a genuine liquefaction event, not of the frequency condition alone. Or it suggests the Lapidary's induced frequency conditions are insufficient. The Lapidary does not know which.

What the Lapidary would need to produce the cross-fragment resonance artificially: a simultaneous full liquefaction event across all nine registered fragments. The Lapidary has not been able to induce a single full event in controlled conditions.

The gap between where the Lapidary is and where it needs to be is large. The Lapidary is aware of this. The Lapidary continues.
